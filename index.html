Who We Are

More than
robotics.

Team 5036, the Robo Devils, are a passionate group of high school students from Sir Wilfrid Laurier Collegiate Institute in Scarborough, Ontario, competing in the FIRST Robotics Competition since 2014.

Every season, we have just six weeks to design, build, program, and test a competition ready robot, an experience built on real engineering, real teamwork, and real grit.

But what we build goes beyond machines. Community is at the core of everything we do. Through mentoring younger students, running STEM workshops, and building strong connections across Scarborough Village, we work to make engineering more accessible and inspire the next generation.
